Communications Specialist in Portland, Oregon

As a writer and illustrator, I create communications for progressive causes. Over the years I adapted my experience creating art with traditional materials to digital tools. I use the Adobe Creative Suite and Japanese software designed for comics and animation called Clip Studio Paint. The software facilitates any changes and improvements to illustration while working with a team. Let's talk about your project and how I might be of service.

I have long worked as an independent contractor and with a community of writers, listening to their advice and helping each other to improve. My experience convening writers groups helps in working with all manner of professionals who must work within written and normative rules to accomplish their goals. But most of all, I love to learn. The discovery of ideas and insights, the experience of new perspectives is the foundation of my work.
